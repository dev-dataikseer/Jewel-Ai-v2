# Building a Jewelry-Only AI Image Generation App

## Core Principle

Jewelry image generation is not the same as general fashion generation.

Users care about:

* Material realism
* Gemstone accuracy
* Reflections and lighting
* Fine detail preservation
* Luxury presentation
* Product consistency
* Manufacturing plausibility
* Brand aesthetics

Your entire system prompt architecture should optimize for:

1. Precision
2. Controlled outputs
3. High visual consistency
4. Accurate jewelry physics/materials
5. Luxury-grade photography aesthetics

---

# 1. Things You Must Keep in Mind

## A. Jewelry Is Extremely Sensitive to Detail

Small mistakes destroy realism instantly.

Common failures:

* Melted prongs
* Asymmetrical earrings
* Wrong gemstone cuts
* Impossible reflections
* Bent chains
* Uneven pavé stones
* Broken clasps
* Warped rings
* Inconsistent metal texture
* Floating gems
* Finger anatomy issues in hand shots

Your prompts must explicitly constrain these.

---

## B. Consistency Is More Important Than Creativity

For jewelry commerce:

* consistency > artistic freedom

Your app should produce:

* repeatable compositions
* predictable lighting
* stable product geometry
* clean backgrounds
* matching style across generations

Avoid:

* random artistic hallucinations
* dramatic fantasy styling
* uncontrolled accessories
* chaotic backgrounds

---

## C. Material Accuracy Is Critical

You must explicitly describe:

### Metals

* 18k yellow gold
* 14k rose gold
* platinum
* sterling silver
* brushed gold
* polished white gold

### Gemstones

* VVS diamond
* emerald cut diamond
* oval sapphire
* pear ruby
* moissanite
* lab-grown diamond

### Surface Details

* high polish
* mirror finish
* satin finish
* brushed texture
* pavé setting
* bezel setting
* prong setting

Without explicit specification, models hallucinate.

---

## D. Lighting Is Everything

Jewelry looks premium because of lighting.

Best lighting styles:

* softbox luxury lighting
* studio product lighting
* diffused macro lighting
* cinematic luxury lighting
* high-end ecommerce photography
* black reflective luxury setup
* white seamless studio lighting

Avoid:

* harsh sunlight
* flat lighting
* cartoonish HDR
* overexposed diamonds

---

## E. Camera Language Matters

Your prompts should include:

* macro photography
* 85mm lens
* 100mm macro lens
* shallow depth of field
* focus stacking
* ultra high detail
* commercial jewelry photography

This massively improves output quality.

---

# 2. Recommended App Architecture

## Separate Prompt Layers

Do NOT use one giant prompt.

Use:

### Layer 1 — System Prompt

Controls:

* safety
* style consistency
* realism
* restrictions
* jewelry-specific rules

### Layer 2 — Product Prompt

User input:

* ring type
* gemstone
* metal
* style
* engraving

### Layer 3 — Photography Prompt

Controls:

* lighting
* camera
* angle
* background
* reflections

### Layer 4 — Negative Prompt

Blocks:

* distorted jewelry
* extra gemstones
* broken geometry
* blurry details
* low quality

This architecture gives far better control.

## Mistake 2 — Ignoring Negative Prompts

Negative prompts are mandatory for jewelry.

---

## Mistake 3 — No Style Locking

Without style consistency:

* every image looks different
* collections fail
* ecommerce looks unprofessional

---

## Mistake 4 — Too Much Creativity

Jewelry customers want:

* luxury realism

Not surreal AI art.
